# Product Context

## Problem Definition
Harshit Chaturvedi needs a professional online presence that:
1. Effectively communicates career transition from Frontend Engineer to Product Manager
2. Showcases both technical expertise and business acumen
3. Differentiates from typical engineering portfolios
4. Attracts PM/APM opportunities from recruiters and hiring managers

## User Experience Goals

### Primary User Journey (Recruiters/Hiring Managers)
1. Land on portfolio → Immediately understand value proposition
2. Scan professional experience → Recognize relevant skills
3. Review credentials → Validate technical + business background
4. Take action → Contact or download resume

### Secondary User Journey (Network/Peers)
1. Explore portfolio → Understand professional capabilities
2. Discover HC BYTES → Learn about education platform
3. Connect on social → Follow on Medium, LinkedIn, YouTube

## Value Proposition
"Frontend Engineer with 5+ years of experience and an MBA in IT. I combine technical expertise with business acumen to deliver products that are feasible, viable, and desirable."

## Key Differentiators
1. **Technical + Business Dual Background**: MBA in IT combined with hands-on engineering
2. **Entrepreneurial Experience**: Running HC BYTES education platform
3. **Research-Oriented**: MBA research on e-commerce platforms
4. **Modern Tech Stack**: ReScript, React, data-driven development
5. **Clear Career Narrative**: Intentional transition from code to strategy

## Content Strategy
- **Hero Section**: Immediate attention with "Bridging the gap between Code & Strategy"
- **About Section**: Position as "Technical Product Manager" with credibility markers
- **Experience Section**: Emphasize cross-functional collaboration and business alignment
- **Research Section**: Demonstrate analytical and strategic thinking
- **Education Section**: Validate formal business training
- **Contact Section**: Clear CTA for PM/APM opportunities

## Design Philosophy
- Clean, professional aesthetic
- Modern animations that demonstrate technical competence
- Information hierarchy optimized for quick scanning
- Trust-building through credentials and specific achievements
- Mobile-first, accessible design

## Success Indicators
- Recruiter spends >2 minutes on site
- Resume download rate
- Contact form submissions / email inquiries
- LinkedIn connection requests post-visit
- Clear understanding of PM value proposition
